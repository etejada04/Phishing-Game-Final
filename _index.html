How to spot a fake website?

Tips

Watch out! Be aware of what you’re clicking on
Phishing often happens via email, messaging apps (whatsApp, Messenger, etc), and social media
Look carefully at the URL before entering any information on the website
  • Secured URL - + HTTPS
  • Url address is not misspelled or unusual
Use common sense - offers too good to be true are usually a scam, and poor grammar and misspelled words are a dead give away
If it looks suspicious, don’t click on it.
Go to the official website of the business you are dealing with
The page only has log in details - missing “Create a new account”, and/or “Forgot your password?”
